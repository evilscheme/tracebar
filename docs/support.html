FAQ

How do I change the target host?

Click the TraceBar menubar icon to open the panel, then open Settings (gear icon or Cmd+,). Enter a hostname or IP address in the Target Host field and press Return.

What do the colors mean?

Colors represent latency. Cool/green tones indicate low latency (fast), while warm/red tones indicate high latency (slow). You can choose from 13 color schemes in Settings and adjust the latency scale threshold.

Why do some hops show asterisks (*)?

Some routers are configured not to respond to ICMP packets. This is normal and does not indicate a problem with your connection.

Does TraceBar need root or admin access?

No. TraceBar uses unprivileged ICMP sockets (SOCK_DGRAM) that work without elevated permissions, fully inside the macOS App Sandbox.

What macOS versions are supported?

TraceBar requires macOS 14.6 (Sonoma) or later.
